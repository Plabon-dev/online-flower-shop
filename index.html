FAQ

How do I place an order for flowers online?

Placing an order on our online flower shop is easy. Simply browse through our selection of flower arrangements, bouquets, or individual flowers, select the ones you like, choose the desired quantity, and add them to your cart. Proceed to the checkout page, provide the necessary information, including the recipient's address and your contact details, and complete the payment process. Once done, your order will be confirmed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and PayPal. At checkout, you'll be able to select the payment option that suits you best.

Can I customize my flower arrangement?

Yes, we offer customization options for flower arrangements. You can specify any particular preferences or requests in the "Special Instructions" box during the checkout process. We will do our best to accommodate your preferences, but please note that certain seasonal flowers or unique requests may be subject to availability.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we provide same-day delivery for orders placed before our designated cut-off time, usually around noon or early afternoon. Please note that same-day delivery is subject to the availability of the chosen flowers and the delivery location. We recommend placing your order as early as possible to ensure timely delivery.
